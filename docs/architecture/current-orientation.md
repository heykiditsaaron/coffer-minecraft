## SER/CER

SER/CER records are accountable contact receipts, not ordinary logs.

They record when a boundary contact was actually received and what minimal
earned detail was justified by that contact.

## Stable Envelope

The stable envelope is:

- `timestamp`
- `interactionId`
- `recordType`
- `stage`
- optional earned detail

## Historical Honesty

Older terminology in this repository may still reflect earlier discovery
states. That is intentional and should not be flattened into a false sense of
finality.

Use the recent geometry and topology journals as the current interpretive lens,
and read older material as the record of how that lens was earned.
